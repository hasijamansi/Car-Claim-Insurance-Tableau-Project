## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing car insurance claim data using Tableau to uncover trends, patterns, and business insights related to customer demographics, vehicle characteristics, policy details, and claim behavior. The objective is to help insurance companies make data-driven decisions by identifying key factors that influence claim frequency and claim amounts.
The analysis includes exploratory data analysis (EDA) through interactive visualizations and dashboards, enabling stakeholders to better understand customer risk profiles, monitor claim performance, and optimize insurance policies. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of claim distributions, customer segments, vehicle categories, and regional trends, helping insurers improve risk assessment and operational efficiency.

## Business Objective

- Analyze car insurance claim patterns and trends.
- Identify customer segments associated with higher claim rates.
- Evaluate the impact of demographic and vehicle-related factors on claims.
- Support data-driven decision-making for risk management and policy optimization.
- Improve customer profiling and insurance pricing strategies.

## Key Insights

- Identified customer groups with higher claim likelihood.
- Analyzed the relationship between vehicle characteristics and claim amounts.
- Highlighted trends in insurance claims across different customer segments.
- Provided actionable insights for improving underwriting and risk assessment processes.

## Business Impact

The dashboard enables insurance companies to monitor claim behavior, identify high-risk segments, and make informed decisions regarding policy pricing, customer retention, and risk management strategies.
